Frequently Asked Questions

Who's the perfect fit for Morningside Automation?

A brilliant question! Morningside Automation caters to entrepreneurs and small to medium businesses aiming to bulletproof their operations through AI. Our bespoke automations, devoid of hefty custom code costs, can seamlessly integrate into your business in just a few days.

Who won't benefit from Morningside Automation?

Morningside Automation may not suit those who are looking to develop web applications or require intricate, custom-coded AI solutions. If you're on the fence about whether we're the right fit for you, feel free to book a call with us.

Do I need to know what automations I need, or can you help identify the best ones?

If you've already envisioned the automations you want, that's great! But if not, no worries at all. Numerous businesses have vast untapped potential for optimization through our automation systems. Our team will help you discover these during our initial workshop call and assist you in prioritizing the ones that matter most.

Can I request unlimited automations?

Once you're subscribed, feel free to queue as many automation requests as you'd like. We'll deliver them sequentially, one after the other.

What's the turnaround time for my automation requests?

Each automation request you add to the ideas board comes with an estimated implementation time. Depending on complexity, this can range from a couple of days to nearly half a month.

Who's on the development team?

Morningside Automation was born out of a thriving community of over 50,000 AI enthusiasts. We have access to the cream of the crop globally when it comes to hiring. The team you engage with is a result of this selective process.

How do I go about requesting automations?

Morningside Automation offers ample flexibility in how you can request automations. Trello, our platform of choice, allows for a variety of submission methods. You can share Google Docs, wireframes, or even a brief Loom video if you're not a fan of writing out briefs. If it can be linked to or shared in Trello, it's all systems go

What if I only need a single automation?

No problem at all. You can pause your subscription once we've addressed your immediate need, and reactivate it whenever you have more automation requirements. You won't have to worry about the remaining days of your subscription going to waste.

Is there a refund policy if I'm unsatisfied with the service?

We provide a no questions asked money back guarantee up to 15 days after purchasing our service.
